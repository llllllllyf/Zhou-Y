Before You Cast

The I Ching responds to sincerity

  • Quiet your mind. Breathe deeply and release distracting thoughts.
  • Sincerity is the key. The oracle speaks only to those who approach with true intention.
  • Do not divine idly. Ask only when the matter truly moves your heart.
  • Once you ask, do not doubt. Trust the answer you receive, for a divided heart sees nothing clearly.
  • Respect the hexagram. Approach this ancient wisdom with reverence, not as a game.
